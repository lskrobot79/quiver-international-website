About Quiver International

Building stronger communities through holistic development

Quiver International is a nonprofit organization dedicated to equipping individuals, families, and communities worldwide. We believe that lasting change happens when people are empowered to grow spiritually, physically, economically, and socially.

Through our comprehensive programs, we provide education, training, wellness initiatives, and community development resources to underserved populations. Our approach is holistic—addressing the whole person and the whole community.

We work alongside local partners to create sustainable impact that transforms lives and builds thriving communities.

Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives for community transformation

Baseball Development

Youth baseball training programs that build character, teamwork, and athletic skills. We use America's pastime to teach discipline, perseverance, and leadership while providing equipment and coaching to underserved communities.

Women's Functional Fitness

Empowering women through functional fitness training that builds strength, confidence, and community. Our programs focus on practical movements that enhance daily life, promote wellness, and create supportive networks of women encouraging one another.

Educational Media & Content

We develop and distribute educational content including podcasts, training videos, online courses, and written materials on leadership, business, and personal development.

Leadership & Business Training

We provide business development workshops, leadership skills training, organizational capacity building, and sustainability training for individuals and organizations.

Health & Wellness

We promote community health through fitness and wellness coaching, nutrition education, and vocational training in food preparation and healthy cooking.

Literacy & Language Development

We support language preservation and literacy through translation projects, literacy program development, and educational materials for underserved language communities.

Technology Services

We provide technology infrastructure and support to other charitable organizations, including website development, database solutions, and technology training.

Family & Community Development

We support holistic family and community development through parenting education, community programs, and spiritual and personal development resources.

Our Impact

Creating lasting change in communities

Our name reflects our mission: to fill communities with equipped, empowered individuals who can make a positive impact in the world around them.

We believe every person has the potential to create change. Through education, training, and support, we help unlock that potential and build stronger families and communities.
